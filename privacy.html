Meal Planner Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Meal Planner handles information when you use the app and related support pages.

Information You Provide

Meal Planner may process information you choose to add to the app, such as recipes, meal plans, grocery items, support requests, and account details used for sign-in or support.

Camera Access

Meal Planner may request access to your device camera if you choose to import a recipe by taking a photo. Camera access is optional and is only used when you start a camera-based recipe import inside the app.

How Information Is Used

Information handled by Meal Planner is used to provide core app features such as saving recipes, planning meals, organizing grocery lists, importing recipe content, responding to support requests, and maintaining your account.

Sharing

Meal Planner does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to operate the app, respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, or protect the app and its users.

Your Choices

You can choose whether to grant optional permissions such as camera access. If you contact support about privacy or account deletion, send your request to meal.planner.support@proton.me.

Contact

For privacy questions, account deletion requests, or other support needs, email meal.planner.support@proton.me.
